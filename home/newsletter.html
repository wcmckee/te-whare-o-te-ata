Newsletter

Newsletter - printable

The bi-monthly Fairfield Community Newsletter provides information on events happening at Te Whare o te Ata and in the Fairfield Chartwell area. 

It is currently hand delivered to 1700 homes in the area around Te Whare o te Ata.  We can also either email a copy to you.
 Please email tewhareoteata@fairfield.org.nz or phone us  855 7804 and tell us you want to receive a copy of the newsletter.

Or alternatively you can view a copy here, just click the link below to view and print a copy for yourself.
