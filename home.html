Welcome to Te Whare O Te Ata

Te Whare O te Ata Community House in Fairfield was established in 1986 to support and affirm the Fairfield/Chartwell Community.  It is a large house with open plan lounge, dining and kitchen area that is suitable for up to 30 people, and a smaller room suitable for up to five visitors. The ten place computer suite is available for individuals or group bookings

There is off street parking for 12 cars. The house has free Wi-Fi, a free public phone for local calls and free Internet access in the lounge and computer suite.

 Hire charge is set at a very low level for community groups to encourage community participation. We are able to do this because  Hamilton City Council
supports our rental costs.
